My Flower Animation

By Nahala Famia

This animation tells the simple story of a flower growing from a seed to full bloom. I created it using Adobe Illustrator and After Effects as part of my final project. The idea was inspired by my childhood memories of gardening with my sisters. This piece reflects how growth takes time, care, and patience.
